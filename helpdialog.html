Tips & things to try

close
Note: Works best in Google Chrome & MS Edge, Safari does not support WebAudio fully yet.
  • Adjust your volume then click on the speaker in the top right to hear the wave @ 220hz
  • Try sliding the n slider or use your arrow keys to change how many terms there are
  • Select the middle switch to hear only one term at a time, then toggle it back to hear that one frequency help form the overall wave
  • Try pausing the simulation, then stepping with the S key (or the button) when theta is 0 or pi.
  • Listen to how the ringing goes away when the Lanczos sigma factor is added and notice how the wave is actually louder
  • Turn on the overlay components feature to see all of the waves being added together
  • Try comparing what the equations look like between the different waves
  • Adjust the period of the wave to compare the sounds the Square and Sawtooth wave make
You can open this tips window at any time by pressing the question mark in the upper right hand corner
